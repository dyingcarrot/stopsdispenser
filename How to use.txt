Put your discord token in token.txt (if you don't have one follow this tutorial: https://github.com/reactiflux/discord-irc/wiki/Creating-a-discord-bot-&-getting-a-token)

Put your accounts in the textfiles.

When starting the program it will ask you for a key, you can find it in my nulled thread.

commands are:

!spotify - generates a spotify account
!netflix - generates a netflix account
!hulu - generates a hulu account
!stock - shows the stock for each account type
!help - shows commands and info
!clear - clears the screen of the bot, removes log and restarts logging
